Case Study

Lamera – Mobile App UX/UI Case Study

OverView:

A mobile-first restaurant application designed to help users easily browse the menu, place orders, and enjoy a smooth food ordering experience.

The Challenge:

Users needed a fast and intuitive way to explore the restaurant menu and complete orders without confusion or unnecessary steps on mobile devices.

The Goal:

Design a clean, user-friendly mobile app that simplifies the ordering process and enhances the overall user experience.

My Role:

UX Research , User Flow , Wireframing , UI Design , Prototyping.

Tools:

Figma , Google Forms.

Research & Insights:

  • Users prefer quick access to menu categories
  • Clear food images increase trust and engagement
  • A short and simple checkout flow improves usability

User Flow:

Open App → Browse Menu → Select Items → Add to Cart → Checkout.

Design Solution:

  • Mobile-first layout
  • Clear and visible CTA buttonst
  • Warm color palette suitable for food apps
  • Strong visual hierarchy and readability

OutCome:

✔️ Faster ordering experience

✔️ Clean and engaging interface

✔️ Improved usability on mobile devices

Key Learnings:

This project helped me strengthen my mobile UX thinking and create user-centered designs focused on simplicity and clarity.
